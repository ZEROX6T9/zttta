Orion Nebula
I took my first nebula photo. It's so bad but it is a golden picture for me... The Orion Nebula is a star nursery located in the constellation Orion, known for its bright and active star formation. It is one of the most intense areas of stellar formation within the Milky Way. Visible to the naked eye, it contains the young Trapezium cluster whose massive stars illuminate the nebula.

Pleiades – Seven Sisters
The Seven Sisters, known as the Pleiades, is a prominent star cluster in the constellation Taurus... The Pleiades is an open star cluster with over 1,000 stars, located about 410 light-years away. Easily visible to the naked eye, it has inspired myths across cultures and is a favorite target for astrophotographers.

Andromeda Galaxy
Capturing our closest galactic neighbor – the magnificent Andromeda Galaxy... Andromeda (M31) is the nearest spiral galaxy to the Milky Way, 2.5 million light-years away. It contains over a trillion stars and is on a future collision course with our galaxy.
